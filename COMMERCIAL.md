## What counts as commercial use

The PolyForm Noncommercial license follows the definition in the license text:
any use that is not a noncommercial purpose as defined there. In practice this
includes using eigenheim inside a company to support revenue-generating work,
embedding it in a product or service you sell, or distributing it as part of a
commercial offering.
